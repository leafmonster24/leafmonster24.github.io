The Journey

The whole process in my swimming journey.

The beginning

 

I start swimming when I was around 5, just for my hobby. At that moment I really do not practice much. However, I start to take some training course when I was 12. The reason is because I have join the primary school inter-school swimming competition. But I am the last one who finish it. Also as you can figure out that I am really very fat at that time, I want to through swimming to loose some weight.

Start getting improvement

Therefore, I had started the regular train, it was really tough. I need to swim more that 3000 meters in one training section, so it make me felt very tired. However, after a few mouth training my waistline decrease from 32 inch to 26 inch. So I feel really shock about that. In this photo you can see How much effort I pay.

here you can see the changes after a year, I became more stronger. Furthermore I start to won some price too.

Earn a slightly success

When I was around 13 or 14, I won 2 gold and 3 slive that also my first time won a championship. Even it is just my school competition, it means a lot to me. At that moment, I think that if I keep it up, I might have a chance to be professional swimmer. Since some of te reason I can't do it

the frustration and decision

However, I stop all the training after that inter-school competition. Because I am really frustrating and loose my passion on swimming. I start feeling this when I was form 3, because I have found out that I did not have a much talent in this aspect. I am not tall enough. My height increase very slow after form 2 and I figure out that I have no chance to be a professional player, therefore why I need to work that hard?

I never win a match in high level. No matter how much effort I pay I can't be professional. Moreover, I need to care about my academic side too. This make me straggly for almost half year. Finally, I made the decision in that summer, I would too give it up after the inter-school championship in October.
