A throwback reflection in my own journey..

The reflection

There is a lot think I can mention in my swimming journey, even in my journey I cannot mention everything. However, I can pick something to talk about it. In my short swimming career, it seems to be not that successful, there is a lot of high and low. I have face a lot of difficulty. I have overcome it one by one. I used to care about the honor too much and it make me feel perusable and not too comfortable. I did not enjoy swimming. Every day keep on doing the same thing, just train, train and train. So I think it makes me feel unhappy and start to hate it. Except winning the game, I think swimming can have other purpose which I never care about it in the past, such as, just keep yourself healthy and make more friends.

I will go to swimming now when I have spare time. It makes me feel relax. No one can disturb me inside the pool, there is not much noise, so peaceful. That was something I never experience before. I found out that I have miss so many things in the past time, I care about the price too much. So I think I am not giving swimming, I am just using a new aspect too it only.
